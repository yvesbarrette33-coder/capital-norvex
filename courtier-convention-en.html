2. PURPOSE OF THE AGREEMENT

This agreement (the "Agreement") sets out the terms under which the Broker may, as a referral partner, submit private real-estate financing files to Capital Norvex on behalf of his or her clients.

Capital Norvex remains the sole judge of the acceptance, refusal, structure and terms of any financing granted to a client referred by the Broker. This Agreement does not create an exclusive book-of-business arrangement nor a representation mandate.

3. PARTNER BROKER NUMBER

Capital Norvex assigns the Broker a personal and unique partner broker number in the format CN-YYYY-NNN. This number is mandatory for any client file submission to the Capital Norvex platform.

Any submission made without a valid broker number will be rejected, and the analysis fees paid by the client will not be refunded. The Broker undertakes to keep the number confidential and not to share it with any third party.

4. BROKER COMPENSATION

Capital Norvex's file fees (3% to 3.5% of the financed amount) are collected by the notary (in Quebec) or the closing lawyer (in other applicable Canadian jurisdictions) at closing and paid exclusively to Capital Norvex. These fees do not cover the Broker's compensation.

The Broker's compensation is negotiated on a case-by-case basis for each referred file, based on the size of the loan, complexity, file quality, and value brought by the Broker, in addition to Capital Norvex's fees. The Broker submits an invoice to Capital Norvex prior to closing. At closing, the closing officer (notary or lawyer, depending on the jurisdiction) deducts this compensation from the proceeds of the approved financing and pays it directly to the Broker, with no separate disbursement required from the client beyond the financing itself.

The percentage retained for a given file is agreed upon between Capital Norvex and the Broker prior to the issuance of the letter of intent (LOI), disclosed in the LOI signed by the client who confirms acceptance. No compensation is owed to the Broker if the financing is not closed at the closing officer designated by Capital Norvex. Neither party is obligated to continue the relationship: neither the Broker nor Capital Norvex is required to accept or refer any given file.

5. FILE FEES — EXCLUSIVE TO CAPITAL NORVEX

Capital Norvex collects, from the notary at closing, file fees of 3% to 3.5% of the financed amount. These fees are paid exclusively to Capital Norvex and may under no circumstance be charged against the Broker's commission or shared with the Broker.

6. NO ADDITIONAL COMMISSION

The Broker acknowledges that he or she is compensated solely under the grid set out in Article 4. On the same file, the Broker will not add any additional fee to the client (referral fee, administrative fee, premium, management fee, etc.) — the Article 4 grid is complete and final.

This pricing transparency is one of the strong commitments of the Capital Norvex broker program toward clients. The Broker may discuss this at any time with his or her dedicated agent if needed.

7. EXCLUSIVITY — ONLY AFTER THE COMMITMENT LETTER IS SIGNED

Before the commitment letter is signed: no exclusivity is imposed. The Broker, like the client, remains free to shop around and compare our offer with that of other lenders. Our goal is to earn your trust through the quality and speed of our analysis — not through a restrictive clause.

Once the commitment letter has been signed by the client, and to allow Capital Norvex to mobilize its notarial resources and structure the final financing, the file is handled exclusively by Capital Norvex for a period of thirty (30) days. During this period, the Broker agrees not to submit the same file to another private lender. After 30 days, if closing has not occurred for reasons beyond the Broker's or the client's control, the exclusivity ends automatically.

If any difficulty arises during this exclusivity period, Capital Norvex and the Broker will discuss it openly to find a fair resolution. The objective remains collaboration and clarity between the parties.

8. BROKER WORKSPACE 24/7 & CONFIDENTIALITY

The Broker has access to a personal Broker Workspace, available 24/7 on desktop and mobile (installable application). It provides real-time tracking of each referred file (stage, status, approved amount, expected commission, estimated disbursement date) and delivers the Letter of Intent (LOI) ready to present to the client.

To respect client confidentiality, internal analyses (Score Norvex, Norvex Intel, credit committee reports, management notes) remain for the exclusive use of Capital Norvex. They are never shared automatically with the Broker, except when management specifically decides to forward a particular report for the needs of the file.

The Broker undertakes not to disclose to any third party the information communicated by Capital Norvex regarding an active file, except with the prior written consent of the client concerned.

9. DEDICATED COMMUNICATION CHANNEL

The Broker Workspace is the primary channel for tracking files: dashboard, submission of new clients, download of LOIs, real-time status. For any complex question or specific request, the Broker also benefits from a dedicated follow-up agent, a human point of contact for tailored exchanges.

Before notarial signing, all file-related exchanges (instructions, documents, disbursements, releases, schedule) are centralized between Capital Norvex and the notary. The Broker addresses questions to his or her dedicated agent, who responds promptly; the Broker does not need to communicate directly with the notary during this phase.

The Broker submits the commission invoice through the Broker Workspace or to the dedicated agent. Capital Norvex then forwards the invoice to the notary together with the complete financing file, so that payment is ready at the time of signing.

The Broker is welcome at the notarial signing to collect his or her commission payment in person; at that point the Broker may interact freely with the notary in the usual manner.

10. GOOD FAITH AND COMPLIANCE OBLIGATIONS

The Broker undertakes to:

  • Submit only complete, accurate and good-faith files;
  • Maintain a valid practice license at all times (OACIQ — Quebec or FSRA — Ontario);
  • Comply with all applicable laws and regulations, including those governing mortgage brokerage and consumer protection;
  • Promptly notify Capital Norvex of any suspension, revocation, modification or expiry of the license;
  • Refrain from any representation that could mislead a client as to the financing terms, review timelines or chances of acceptance.

11. TERM AND TERMINATION

The Agreement takes effect on the date of electronic signature and remains in force for an initial term of twenty-four (24) months, automatically renewable for successive periods of twenty-four (24) months.

Either party may withdraw at any time upon a simple written notice of thirty (30) days, without justification or penalty. Files in progress at the date of withdrawal are carried through to closing under the terms of the Agreement, in order to protect the clients and the commission already earned by the Broker.

In the event of a serious breach of the obligations under the Agreement (notably Articles 5, 6, 7, 8, 9 or 10), Capital Norvex may terminate the Agreement early, after a prior discussion with the Broker where circumstances permit.

12. AMENDMENT

Capital Norvex may amend the terms of the Agreement (notably the commission grid) upon thirty (30) days' written notice. Failing written objection from the Broker within that period, the amendments are deemed accepted and binding on the Broker.
